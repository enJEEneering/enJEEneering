How to Prepare for NTSE Stage 1

By Anonymous

You can prepare for NTSE exam along with regular classes as the syllabus of NTSE is based on NCERT class 9 and 10th syllabus. It’s imperative to thoroughly study the Social Studies NCERT book for NTSE. Strengthen Your MAT Section. The MAT section tests your mental ability. Hence, you must give sufficient time for preparing for this part.the candidates need to check the last years’ NTSE papers, and they can evaluate where they stand. Simultaneously, they can analyze their strengths and weaknesses based on their existing preparedness and the required NTSE preparation. This will help the candidates decide how much weightage they have to give to various sections under the limited timeframe they have for NTSE preparation. Candidates can then utilize their strength and make a strategy to improve their weaknesses during the NTSE preparation. social science has a large portion and there is much to remember in that so you can make proper and precise notes for that which you can read and refer at last moment. one must look at all maps and diagrams given in ncert books and remember important things. there is no negative marking in paper so one must answer all questions.
MAT requires practice and one must be thorough with all concepts and even some short tricks.there are several topics like calender test,Venn Diagrams,Paper Cutting and Folding,Water and Mirror Images , Blood Relations , Alphabet and Number Test , Ranking and Arrangements , Coding-Decoding , Distance and Direction , Verbal and Non Verbal Analogy. practice ample number of questions on each topic to be comfortable with them and then one can score in them easily.
Remember all updated statistics and numbers in social science ncert books.
Do even look at diagrams in biology and study every chapter carefully noticing and highlighting important points.Do not go beyond ncert books .Rest of syllabus is same as that of ncert. Do solve ample number of papers to get acquainted with pattern and one can use timer . Preparing for ntse also helps in 10 th exams and also gives one a feel of a national level examination. one can even refer to some online resources.
