RESPONDING TO THE DRAFT ANSWER KEYS

1. The purpose of releasing the draft answer keys is to report any discrepancy or disagreement with the provided answers.
2. DO NOT provide comment to a particular answer key (example ABC) without proper justification. Provide the complete solution with diagrams if required, justifying your alternate answer.
3. Please indicate the question number as it is numbered on the uploaded UCEED QP.
4. As far as possible, put all your comments into a single email rather than multiple emails for different questions.
5. The UCEED office will not respond to your emails. All your emails, however, will be carefully read and issues raised will be thoroughly checked.
