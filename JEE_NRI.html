How Can NRI students apply and prepare for JEE

By Anonymous

Introduction

There are many NRI students studying outside India who like to apply and prepare for IIT-JEE for admission in prestigious institutes like IITs and NITs. Here we are answering frequently asked questions of NRI students and parents regarding JEE eligibility, admission process and exam preparation.

Can NRI students write IIT JEE entrance exam?
Yes, NRI students can write IIT JEE entrance exam, provided they fulfill all the eligibility criteria like age limit, appearance and performance in Grade XII (or equivalent) examination, etc. for JEE Main and JEE Advanced.
Is there any NRI quota in IIT? No, there is no NRI quota in IITs. NRI students, just like Indian Nationals, have to clear the cut-off for JEE Advanced and admission will happen on the basis of their ranks. Students need to first qualify JEE Main and then clear JEE Advanced for admission in IITs.
Can NRI students write JEE Main? Yes, NRI students can write JEE Main exam which is the qualifying exam for JEE Advanced. Students can give JEE Main exam twice in a year in the month of January and April.
How do NRI students get admission in NITs? NRI students can get admission in NITs in two ways : 1) By giving JEE Main and clearing the exam, 2) By applying through DASA scheme (admissions on the basis of SAT and SAT 2 subjects score).
Is there an NRI quota for studying in NITs? Not exactly an NRI quota, NITs have some reserved seats for NRI students under the DASA scheme. The number of seats in DASA quota is 15% of the total seats in the institute. Currently admissions in DASA/ CIWG seats happens on the basis of SAT scores, but from 2021, it will be on the basis of JEE Main rank only.
How can NRI students prepare for the JEE exam? NRI students can join online coaching for JEE preparation where they will get live classes, hard copy study material, DPPs and online test series. Check out Top 15 Preparation Tips for JEE Main and JEE Advanced.
Can NRI students get admission in NITs through JEE Main or only DASA? Yes, NRI students can get admission in NITs by directly applying for JEE Main and clearing the exam, and not just through DASA scheme. From 2021-22 onwards, DASA admissions will happen on the basis of JEE Main ranks instead of SAT scores.
What is the fee for NRI students in NITs? The fee for NRI students in NITs is same as for students in India if you got admission through JEE Main. However, if selected under DASA scheme, the fee would be higher than the normal fee. CIWG candidates will have to pay the same fee prescribed for Indian National.

Students have to fill the JEE Main online application form at NTA JEE Main website. JEE Advanced registration starts after the JEE Main April results.
How can an NRI student crack JEE exam? An NRI student can crack JEE exam by studying hard with consistency and smart work. For proper guidance and resolving all the doubts in JEE, students can join online coaching platform like Mystudycart.
What are the documents required for JEE examination from an NRI student? While applying for JEE, the documents required from NRI students are scanned clear passport photograph and signature, Grade 10 and 12 or equivalent qualifying exam mark-sheet and one ID proof. At the time of JEE exam, students need to bring Admit Card and ID proof only.
